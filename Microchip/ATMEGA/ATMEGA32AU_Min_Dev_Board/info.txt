Features:
* All 32 I/O pins are pulled out (PA, PB, PC, PD)
* Crystal socket hole. Default is 8MHz.
* Supported chips: ATmega16 MCU / ATmega32 chip is pin compatible.
* Power: power adapter or external expansion pin power supply (not supported by ISP download interface)
* Power Supply 5V (DC-005 Jack 5.5mm * 2.1mm).
* 4 channels VCC, GND.
* Reset: Power-on and reset button.
* Power indicator (D1) and program run indicator (D2).
* Standard ISP download interface.
